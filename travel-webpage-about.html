Like millions of people around the world, I was unprepared for the insurmountable obstacle that is Covid-19. The travel industry was a viable career path and I was ready to sign my name in blood if it meant I could spend the rest of my life planting the seed of travel inside others' hearts. I thought I had finally found my true calling. All these years spent roaming around borders anchorless, would finally be useful and valuable.

Nonetheless, life has its own plan. In March 2020, I was quarantined in a small studio for months. Jobless, hopeless and unable to walk outside longer than 60 minutes; I scrolled through my personal travel videos. Like many people at the time, I was mourning a part of my freedom. Angry at everything, I took it upon myself to learn programming because I thought if there was one thing the world could no longer function without is the Internet and the devices that surround us.

This is a personal project, a testament to my willingness and ability to dance through the winds of change.
All the content as well as the webpage itself is my own work. I hope to inspire, but most importantly I hope it encourages you, the reader, to rediscover yourself and redefine your talents, skills and sense of self.
