Like millions of people around the world, I was unprepared for the insurmountable obstacle that is Covid-19.

I was in the process of becoming a customer advisor for a luxury travel agency and was ready to sign my name in blood if it meant I could spend the rest of my life living my wildest wanderlust dreams.

I thought I had finally found my true calling. All these years spent roaming around the world, meeting a multitude of cultures and navigating a wide variety of social spaces, would finally help me feel useful and valuable.

Nonetheless, life has its own plan. In March 2020, I was confined in a small Parisian studio for months. Jobless, hopeless and unable to walk outside longer than 60 minutes; I watched videos and images of my trip to Venezuela. I was mourning a part of my freedom.

Angry at everything, I took it upon myself to learn programming because I thought if there was one thing the world could no longer function without is the Internet and the devices that surround us.

This is a personal project, a testament to my willingness and ability to dance through the winds of change.

All the content as well as the webpage itself is my own work. I hope to inspire, but most importantly I hope it encourages you, the reader, to rediscover yourself and redefine your talents, skills and sense of self.
